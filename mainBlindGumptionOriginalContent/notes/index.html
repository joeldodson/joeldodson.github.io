Notes From BlindGumption

The notes section is a place for me to keep track of how I've done things, resources I've found useful along the way, and general thoughts I might want to record throughout this journey.

I spent a lot of time thinking of how to structure these notes. I decided I'd better just pick a structure and go with it. So notes are broken down into three categories:

  • Developing and Running BlindGumption -- this will contain notes regarding all elements of getting BlindGumption going, and keeping it going. Click through for more details.
  • Thoughts from Me, BlindGumption -- this is where I'll record various thoughts I've had along this journey. Thoughts will include brief explanations of why I chose a particular solution or maybe some reflections on life in general. No politics. Well, maybe some dry social commentary if it's begging to be written.
  • Resources I Find Helpful -- This might end up being one long index.html file though there might be some articles. It's mostly a place for me to record links of places I find interesting and useful. I'll add some text to explain very briefly why a link is included and you can decide whether it's worth clicking through.
Disclaimer: all thoughts, opinions, and attempts at humor on BlindGumption and associated sites are mine. I do not speak for the blind community.
